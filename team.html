Your Smile Is in Expert Hands

Every member of our team holds advanced qualifications and attends 50+ hours of continuing education each year to stay at the forefront of modern dentistry.

Lead Dentist & Clinical Director

With 15 years of experience in comprehensive dental care, Dr. Priya leads PureSmile Dental's clinical team. She is known for her patient-first approach and meticulous attention to detail in every procedure.

Dr. Rohan Mehta

Cosmetic Dentistry Specialist

Dr. Rohan specialises in smile makeovers, teeth whitening, and porcelain veneers. He has transformed over 1,000 smiles and trained under leading cosmetic dentists in Mumbai and abroad.

BDS, AFMC Pune Diploma Aesthetic Dentistry Invisalign Certified

Dr. Anjali Desai

Paediatric Dentist (Pedodontist)

Dr. Anjali is our child dentistry expert and the warmest member of the team. She has a magical ability to put even the most nervous children completely at ease, making their dental visits genuinely enjoyable.

BDS, Symbiosis Pune MDS Pedodontics Child Behaviour Certified

Dr. Vikram Joshi

Orthodontist & Implantologist

Dr. Vikram manages all braces, aligner, and dental implant cases. With advanced training in digital smile design and 3D implant planning, he delivers predictable, long-lasting results with minimal discomfort.

BDS, MDS Orthodontics ITI Fellow (Implants) 3D Digital Smile Design

Dr. Sneha Kulkarni

Periodontist (Gum Specialist)

Dr. Sneha specialises in gum disease treatment, deep cleaning, and gum surgeries. She believes that healthy gums are the foundation of a healthy smile and works closely with our other specialists on complex cases.

BDS, Govt Dental College Nagpur MDS Periodontics Laser Dentistry Certified

Ms. Kavita Patil

Head Dental Hygienist

Kavita manages preventive care, professional cleanings, and patient education at PureSmile. Her meticulous scaling and polishing work is the reason our patients always leave with their teeth feeling brand new.

Diploma Dental Hygiene 10+ Years Experience Oral Health Educator
What Drives Us

Our Team's Core Values

These are not just words on a wall — they shape every decision, every treatment, and every interaction at PureSmile Dental.

❤️

Genuine Compassion

We remember that behind every tooth is a person with fears, hopes, and a busy life. We treat every patient the way we would treat our own family.

🎯

Clinical Excellence

Good enough is never good enough. We invest in the latest technology, techniques, and training so that every procedure delivers the best possible outcome.

💬

Honest Communication

We explain everything in plain language. No dental jargon, no overselling, no hidden costs. You always know exactly what you are getting and what it will cost.

⏰

Respect for Your Time

We run on schedule. If we are ever delayed, we will tell you in advance. We know your time is valuable and we never make you wait without reason.

🛡️

Safety First

Hospital-grade sterilisation, single-use instruments where required, digital X-rays with minimal radiation, and OSHA-compliant infection control protocols.

🌱

Lifelong Relationships

We are not just here for your emergency. We want to be your dental home for life — for you, your children, and your parents. Prevention is always our priority.
